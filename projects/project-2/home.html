    • TRES LECHES CAKE
    By Fausta Gunawan, Published on Oct 10, 2025

    Tres leches cake is a classic Latin American dessert made with a light sponge soaked in a mix of three milks (evaporated, condensed, and heavy cream). This gives it a rich, moist texture that's both sweet and satisfying. Despite its indulgent flavor, it's easy to make with simple ingredients. It’s perfect for birthdays, holidays, or any special occasion.

    What makes this cake unique is its balance of richness and lightness. The sponge absorbs the milk mixture without getting soggy, creating a soft, melt-in-your-mouth bite. Topped with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon or fruit, it’s as beautiful as it is delicious. This easy recipe is sure to impress. One bite, and you’ll see why it’s a favorite around the world.
    Ingredients

    Cake:

    • Betty Crocker’s Super Moist
    • Yellow Cake Mix
    • 9”x13” Baking Pan

    Tres Leches:

    • 1 can (14 oz.) of Condensed Milk
    • 1 can (12 oz.) of Evaporated Milk
    • 12 oz. of Milk of Your Choice

    Topping:

    • 1 Pint (16 oz.) of Whipping Cream
    • 3 Tbsp. of White Sugar
    • 1 box of Strawberries (Optional)

    Prep Time:

    ~45 mins

    Bake:

    ~30 mins

    Total Time:

    ~1 hr 15 mins

    Servings:

    12 servings
