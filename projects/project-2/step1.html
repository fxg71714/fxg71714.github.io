    • STEP 1: BAKE THE CAKE

    Ingredients:
    • Betty Crocker’s Super Moist Yellow Cake Mix
    • 3 Medium Eggs
    • 1/3 Cup of Vegetable Oil
    • 1 Cup of Water
    • 9x13-inch baking dish

    To begin making your tres leches cake, start by baking the base using Betty Crocker’s Super Moist Yellow Cake Mix. Preheat your oven to 350°F (or 325°F for dark or nonstick pans) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. In a large bowl, combine the cake mix with 3 medium eggs, 1 cup of water, and 1/3 cup of vegetable oil. Beat the mixture with a hand mixer or whisk for about 2 minutes, until smooth and well blended. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 28–33 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
