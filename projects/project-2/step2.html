    • STEP 2: MIX TRES LECHES

    Ingredients:
    • 1 can (14 oz.) of Condensed Milk
    • 1 can (12 oz.) of Evaporated Milk
    • 12 oz. of Milk of Your Choice

    While the cake bakes, mix the tres leches. In a medium bowl, whisk together 1 can (14 oz.) of condensed milk, 1 can (12 oz.) of evaporated milk, and 12. of milk of your choice oz. Stir until fully combined. Set aside until the cake is baked and slightly cooled. This mixture will soak into the cake for a rich, moist texture.
