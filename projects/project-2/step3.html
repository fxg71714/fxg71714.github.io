    • STEP 3: WHISK THE CREAM

    Ingredients:
    • 1 Pint (16 oz.) of Whipping Cream
    • 3 Tbsp. of White Sugar
    • OPTIONAL 1 Box of Strawberries

    Let’s start preparing the topping. In a large bowl, add 1 pint of heavy whipping cream and 3 tbsp. of white sugar. Whisk on high speed for 2–3 minutes, or until soft peaks form. Keep the whipped cream chilled until you're ready to spread it over the cake.

    OPTIONAL STEP: Cut the Strawberries vertically, creating a heart shape.
